About Me

Hi! I'm Peritian, 39 years old, currently living in Argentina. I love drawing as a hobby as much as for work, And despite I enjoy drawing nsfw content the most, I'm open to anything.

I have been drawing since I have memory but I started to take it seriously when I was 14. From then on, since my first DA account, through my FA account and commission work, I've tavelled a long path. I'm really happy that I learned a lot of important things about drawing and comic industry, character designs and general art knoweledge.

I've participated in long term projects for Hardblush, Wetblush, and Clubstripes, and other adult-anthro theme paysites, since I've always enjoyed adult work. Now I'm happy to share this comic with you, which started as a personal project and has now come to life. Supporters on current and former platforms are the ones who made this posible and thanks to them I can dedicate more time to this project, I can produce more art and make my worlds come true.

Thank you so much, and I hope you enjoy and keep supporting me in the future.

About This Comic

Arno and Celio were originally born in Hardblush, as main protagonists of a pinup with a summer-ish theme. Since I liked their design and the fact that they were a pair of interesting characters back then, I decided to keep drawing them in more pinups and vignettes. After drawing them for some time, I wanted them to have their own personal story, and that will be my first attempt to do a long-run project, with a story that may continue beyond a one-shot and more than 10 pages. A lot of characters and situations added to this will end in something interesting, I hope! and I'll do my best to make it true. I hope you enjoy it!
